						 Green path Colombo 
						 

	 Green path Colombo, formerly Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha street that popular for open air events and festive. In every weekend in here maintain art exhibitions. “Kala Pola” is a best example for art exhibition maintain in here. You can buy some paintings in here. Many Sri Lankan artist perfomed their talent in here.  

 

	Food festival also a cultural festival that annually held on here. You can taste Sri Lankan cultural foods. 

 
						 More 
						 

	This street located near to Colombo Art Gallery.  

 

	Green-path or Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha is a broad two-way traffic road that outlines the southern end of Viharamadevi park.
