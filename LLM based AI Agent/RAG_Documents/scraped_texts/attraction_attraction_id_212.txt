						 Pettah Market 
						 

	You can buy anything you want. Pettah Market is one of Largest market in Sri Lanka. In here you can find out one productions in different choices. Watches and jewelery, shoes, bags, suitcases, clothing, cell phones, toys, electronics, souvenirs, spices such as turmeric, nutmeg to cocoa or black cumin, Etc. You get all the spices for a delicious Sri Lankan curry to cook. 

 

	You have to be careful for buying some electronic and jewelleries, watches etc. because these all brands are fake but you can use that items one or two years. Advantage of buying items in Pettah, is cheap Price. You have to bargain for that.  

 
						 More 
						 

	The main entrance to Pettah Market is the Khan Clock Tower in the middle of a roundabout. You can hire a Tuk Tuk going place to another place. It would be 50 LKR for 1KM.
