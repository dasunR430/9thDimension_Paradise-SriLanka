						 Arugam Bay 
						 

	Arugam Bay is on the list of the top ten surf points in the world. The best time of the year is between May and November. In Arugam Bay there are three point breaks “The Point”, “Pottuvil Point” and “Crocodile Rock” are within a hour tuk tuk ride from the Hotel, with The Point being visible (15 min walk) from the hotel.
