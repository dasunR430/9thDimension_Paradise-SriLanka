						 Seema Malakaya Meditation Centre 
						 

	This is a temple that belongs to Gangaramaya temple. This beautiful place designed by one of Sri Lankan best architect Geoffrey Bawa. This temple located in the middle of the lake. This temple covered with a short white wall and on this wall numerous statues of Buddha sitting in different poses. On your visit, stop walking and sit down. Enjoy the calm of being on the water. The hustle and bustle of the city, although close, seems to be a lesson. In the afternoon, this place bright with colourful lights. 

 
						 More 
						 

	This place located near to well-known Ganagaramaya temple. You can hire tuk from here to “Sir James Pieris Mawatha” You will be able to see the water temple from that location.
