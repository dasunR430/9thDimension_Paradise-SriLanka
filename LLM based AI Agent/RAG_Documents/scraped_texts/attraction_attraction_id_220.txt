						 Sri Shakhyasinharamaya 
						 

	This temple inherits to unique past. That is this temple, Sri Shakhyasinharamaya caressed with many Sinhala Buddhist leaders such as Migettuwatte Gunanda Thero and Sir Anagarika Dharmapala. 

 

	Structure of this temple different within other temples. This temple, Sri Shakhyasinharamaya build like a shape of a ship. This ship represents arrival of southern herb of Sri Maha Bodhi and Sanghamitta thero. 

 

	In here you have an able to watch Smadhi Buddha Statue, Recline Buddha Statue, Statues of twenty-eight Buddhas, Relic Palace and many more. When you visit to this place you can free your mind and body. 

 
						 More 
						 

	This temple is situated in Inguru Kade junction at Colombo. When you visit this temple, it might be good to dressed well.
