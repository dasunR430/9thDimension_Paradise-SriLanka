						 Gampaha | Bopagama Water Fall 
						 
						 Bopagama Water Fall 
						 

	 Situated nearly 1.5 km from the Bopagama junction on the Attanagalla –Kirindiwella road the Bopagama water fall is a four meter cascade ideal for pleasure and bath. 

 
						 More 
						 

	 Initiated from the Aquifers of Kandalande Estate the stream is strengthened by the Nagas Canal and cascades from two rock boulders to create the Bopagama falls, then flowing across paddy land to join the Attanagalu tank.  The towering trees of the surrounding forest casts a gentle shadow over the waterfall while the downstream is thrives with local fish like Galpadiy, Pethiya and Dandiy. 

 

	 Things To Do: Natural Bath , Photography , Biking
