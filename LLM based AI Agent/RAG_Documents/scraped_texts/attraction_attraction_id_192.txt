						 Koggala Beach  
						 

	Koggala Beach is one of the longest beach in Sri Lanka.The beach of Koggala is tidy tourist destination.  

 

	The beach is a nesting area for marine turtles who visit the island during the breeding season, Although the opportunity to actually see the laying process is very rare,  

 

	the ancient practice of stilt fishing on the coastlines, may see at Koggala beach. 

 
						 More 
						 

	Koggala Beach is 8 km from Galle City and 130 km from Katunayaka Airport. You can reach to koggala by Road, plane and sea.  

 

	In a Large dry season begin from December to March, and  Small dry season begin from July to August.
