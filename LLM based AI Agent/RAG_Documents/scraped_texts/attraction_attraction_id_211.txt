						 Craft Village 
						 

	Craft village is a most popular tourist destination among both local and foreign tourists. Within this place, you can get a new experience of Sri Lankan rural local industries. In this village, currently have 45 stalls. Clay works, masks, cane, batik, lace works and leather works are popular stalls in this village. 

 

	you can request to make products your liking. Also, you have an able to buy some manufactured products favourable price. 

 
						 More 
						 

	Craft village located in Battaramulla, JanaKala kendraya. It is about 12km from fort Railway station.
