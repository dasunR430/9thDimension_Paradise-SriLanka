						 Huluganga Ella Falls  
						 

	This waterfall is located at Hulu Ganga Town on the Panwila – Bambarella Road from Kandy.Height is about 75 metres and it starts from the Knuckles Range.  

 
						 More 
						 

	Even during heavy rains the water is clear due to rocks, a variety of grasses, etc. At present there is a project to provide water to the people of Kundasale using this water. This also provides a magnificient scenario despite it being located at the centre of a town.
